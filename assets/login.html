Segmentation-free localization of
histology differential attributes

DIANNE provides interactive and static workflows for weakly-supervised spatial classification on whole slide images (H&E, IHC, mIF) and spatial transcriptomics data (Visium, Xenium). Real-time human-in-the-loop labeling enables rapid model training, with inference across whole slide images in under 30 seconds per slide.

This shared environment enables PDXNet members to collaboratively explore PDX H&E and spatial transcriptomics data — annotating, training, and analyzing together in one place.
